Vines To Wines is a unique winery where wine lovers can experience the fun of working in a winery by actually making their own wines. And not just any old wines either, but really good wines, such as Stag's Leap District, Napa Valley Merlot, Sonoma Chardonnay, Columbia Valley Riesling, Lodi Old Vine Zinfandel, Riesling Ice Style Wine, New Zealand Sauvignon Blanc, Italian Valpolicella and Pinot Grigio, and many, many others.

Centrally located in the historic Sherman Hill District of Des Moines, customers go down into the wine cellar of a century old home to make their wines. When the weather permits, wine tastings, and wine makings are done outside, on a patio covered by an Italian style pergola with grape vines, and hundreds of clusters of grapes providing shade. In this unique environment Vines To Wines provides central Iowans with the opportunity to learn about wines, and to have a lot fun in the process, as they become their own wine maker.
